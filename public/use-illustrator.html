Comparison with D3

I have some experience in Adobe Illustrator, sometimes when I build something with code, and encounter some position, size or layout issues, I will just hope I can use the Illustrator to drag this thing to there and rotate that thing by 30 degree. Illustrator is easy to use and can build an eye-catching chart, but it's time-consuming, and nearly not able to re-produce another one with just reading in another data file. In conclusion, if just making one beautiful chart, I will use Illustrator, but when need to live update with data or build interactive chart, it will be D3.
